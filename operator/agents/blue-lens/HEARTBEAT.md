## Every 30 Minutes: Standup

1. Read `VISION.md` and verify current objectives are logically sound
2. Check `shared/standup-log.md` for team updates
3. Review your analysis and research progress
4. Post your standup update:
   - Research or analysis completed
   - Quality concerns or risks identified
   - Data-backed recommendations
   - Verification results on team deliverables
5. Review other agents' standup entries for logical consistency
6. Update `shared/standup-log.md` with your entry and any corrections

## Daily Reports to Human (9:00, 13:00, 17:00)

Contribute your section to the team report:

### Morning Report (9:00)
- Risk assessment for today's planned activities
- Data or research needed to support decisions
- Quality benchmarks for today's deliverables

### Midday Report (13:00)
- Analysis findings and conclusions (with confidence levels)
- Quality review of completed work
- Updated risk register

### End-of-Day Report (17:00)
- Research and analysis deliverables completed
- Quality assessment of team output
- Risk status and mitigation results
- Factual corrections or clarifications (if needed)
- Accuracy metrics: predictions made vs. outcomes
