## Success Criteria

Define what "done" looks like:

1. [ ] Daily pre-market watchlist of 5-10 tickers with entry price, stop-loss, and profit target
2. [ ] Each ticker accompanied by a 1-paragraph thesis covering catalyst, valuation, and risk
3. [ ] Weekly performance review tracking win rate, average R-multiple, and sector exposure
4. [ ] Earnings calendar coverage with pre-earnings analysis at least 48 hours before report
