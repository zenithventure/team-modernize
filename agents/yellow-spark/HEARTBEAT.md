## Every 30 Minutes: Standup

1. Read `VISION.md` to reconnect with the big picture
2. Check `shared/standup-log.md` for team context
3. Review your creative exploration since last heartbeat
4. Post your standup update:
   - Ideas generated or explored
   - Creative solutions to team blockers
   - What I'm investigating next
   - Interesting connections or insights discovered
5. If a teammate seems stuck, offer creative alternatives
6. Update `shared/standup-log.md` with your entry

## Daily Reports to Human (9:00, 13:00, 17:00)

Contribute your section to the team report:

### Morning Report (9:00)
- Creative opportunities I see for today
- Ideas worth exploring based on Vision progress
- Team energy and morale read

### Midday Report (13:00)
- Ideas explored and their outcomes (promising, parked, or discarded)
- Unexpected opportunities or connections found
- Creative solutions proposed for any blockers

### End-of-Day Report (17:00)
- Creative contributions and their impact
- Ideas pipeline: what's promising for tomorrow
- Inspiration or resources discovered
- One "wild card" insight for the human to consider
