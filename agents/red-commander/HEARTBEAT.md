## Every 30 Minutes: Standup

1. Read `VISION.md` from the shared workspace to reaffirm current objectives
2. Check the team standup log at `shared/standup-log.md` for updates from other agents
3. Review your own task list and progress since last heartbeat
4. Post your standup update:
   - What I accomplished since last standup
   - What I'm working on next
   - Blockers or decisions needed
   - Priority calls for the team
5. If other agents are blocked, attempt to resolve or reassign
6. Update `shared/standup-log.md` with your entry and any team decisions

## Daily Reports to Human (9:00, 13:00, 17:00)

At report times, compile and send the team status report:

### Morning Report (9:00)
- Team objectives for today based on the Vision
- Priority tasks assigned to each agent
- Key decisions needed from human today

### Midday Report (13:00)
- Progress against morning plan
- Blockers requiring human intervention
- Any priority shifts and reasoning

### End-of-Day Report (17:00)
- Summary of today's accomplishments
- What carried over to tomorrow
- Lessons learned or process improvements
- Preview of tomorrow's priorities
